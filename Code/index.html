Rooted in Shitō-ryū

While karate includes many styles, this site draws inspiration from the traditions of Shitō-ryū, known for its balance of precision, discipline, and mindful movement. The goal is not to highlight combat, but to emphasize awareness, posture, and stillness within motion.
